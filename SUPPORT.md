# festival Project Community Group - Rules

## 1. Core Principles
Be friendly, inclusive, and rational. Respect all members' technical levels and backgrounds. No trolling, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## 2. Scope of Discussion
Limited to topics related to the **festival Project**, including technical issues, usage tips, contribution collaboration, and version updates. Off-topic chat (e.g., daily gossip, games, financial advice) is prohibited.

## 3. Prohibited Activities
- No advertisements, promotions, QR codes, or external links (including part-time jobs, courses, traffic-driving content). Violators will be removed immediately.
- No vulgar, pornographic, violent, or illegal content, nor sensitive topics such as politics or religion.
- No spamming, flooding, repeated questions, or malicious harassment/stalking of other members.
- No disclosure of others' privacy (e.g., phone numbers, addresses, unpublished information).

## 4. Question Etiquette
Before asking a question, please check the project's `README.md` and `CONTRIBUTING.md` documents. When asking, include **environment version, problem description, and reproduction steps** to improve efficiency.

## 5. Violation Consequences
- First offense: Admin warning and removal of violating content.
- Second offense: Temporary mute for 24 hours.
- Third offense / serious violation (e.g., ads, illegal content): Immediate removal from the group and permanent ban.

## 6. Other Notes
